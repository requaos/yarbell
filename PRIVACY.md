# Privacy Policy for Yarbell

**Effective date:** July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the game **Yarbell** ("the app", package
`com.requaos.yarbell`) handles information. It applies to the app as distributed
through the Google Play Store and elsewhere.

## Summary

**Yarbell does not collect, store, or share any personal data.** The app runs
entirely on your device, works fully offline, and contains no accounts,
analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.

## Information we collect

**None.** The app does not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive
information, including but not limited to:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP addresses
- Location data
- Usage or analytics data
- Photos, files, microphone, or camera data

## Network access

The app does **not** request the Android `INTERNET` permission and makes no
network connections. Nothing you do in the game leaves your device.

## Device permissions

The app requests **no special or sensitive permissions**.

## Local settings

Any in-game preferences (such as brightness and audio volume) are used only to
run the game and, if stored, are kept **locally on your device**. They are never
transmitted or shared, and are removed if you uninstall the app.

## Advertising and third-party services

The app contains **no advertising**, **no analytics**, and **no third-party
SDKs or services**. No data is shared with any third party because no data is
collected.

## Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of
13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). It complies with the
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations by
virtue of collecting no data at all.

## Data security

Because the app collects and transmits no data, there is no personal data at
risk of a breach through the app.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be published in this
document, and the "Effective date" above will be revised accordingly.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at
<https://github.com/requaos/yarbell/issues> or contact the developer at
`reqpro@requaos.com`.
